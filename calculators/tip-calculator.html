Tipping guide — how much to tip

Tipping customs vary by country and service type. In the US, tipping at restaurants is standard and expected. In India and parts of Europe, tipping is appreciated but less mandatory. Use this calculator to find the exact amount for any tip percentage.

🍽️ Restaurants

15–20% is standard in the US. Tip on the pre-tax amount or the full total — the difference is small. Always tip the server, not just the kitchen.

🚗 Ride & delivery

15–20% for ride-share, $3–5 minimum for food delivery or 15–20% of the order. Always tip more for long distances or bad weather.

✂️ Personal services

15–20% for hairdressers, spas, and personal care. Some salons build gratuity in — check before tipping to avoid doubling.

👥 Group bills

Many restaurants add 18% gratuity for groups of 6+. Check the bill first. Use the split feature to confirm each person's fair share.

Frequently asked questions

How much should I tip at a restaurant?
The US standard is 18–20% for good service. 15% for adequate service. 25%+ for exceptional. For a $50 bill at 20%: tip = $10, total = $60. Use this calculator for any amount instantly.
How do I calculate a 20% tip quickly?
Move the decimal one place left to get 10%, then double it for 20%. For $45: 10% = $4.50, 20% = $9.00. Or just enter your bill here and select 20% — done in one second.
How do you split a bill evenly?
Add tip to get the total, then divide by the number of people. This calculator does it automatically — enter the bill, choose the tip %, set the number of people, and each person's share is shown instantly.
Should I tip on the pre-tax or post-tax amount?
Technically, tip on the pre-tax subtotal. In practice, most people tip on the post-tax total since the difference is small. Either approach is acceptable — enter whichever amount is on your receipt.
What currencies does this calculator support?
USD ($), INR (₹), EUR (€), and GBP (£). The tip calculation is the same regardless of currency — just switch the symbol at the top. All amounts display in your chosen currency format.
