How to use the hashtag generator

Enter a topic or keyword — for example "fitness", "food photography", "travel India" — select your platform, choose how many hashtags you want, and click Generate. The tool creates a balanced mix of popular, medium and niche hashtags optimized for your chosen platform.

🎯 Niche hashtags work best

Niche hashtags (under 100K posts) have less competition. Your post is more likely to be discovered by a targeted audience than drowning in millions of posts on #love.

📊 Mix strategy

Use a mix: 3-5 large hashtags for maximum exposure, 5-10 medium ones for good reach, and 5-10 niche ones for targeted discovery. This "hashtag ladder" strategy maximizes visibility.

📷 Instagram tips

Instagram allows 30 hashtags. Post them in your caption or first comment. Avoid banned hashtags. Rotate different hashtag sets to avoid appearing spammy.

🎵 TikTok tips

TikTok's algorithm is less hashtag-dependent. Use 3-5 specific hashtags plus 1-2 trending ones. Keyword-rich captions often matter more than hashtag quantity.

Frequently asked questions

How many hashtags should I use on Instagram?
Instagram allows up to 30 hashtags per post. Recent guidance from Instagram suggests using 3-5 highly relevant hashtags rather than 30 generic ones. For Reels, 3-10 targeted hashtags is recommended for best algorithmic reach.
What is the difference between popular and niche hashtags?
Popular hashtags (1M+ posts) have huge audiences but extreme competition — your post disappears quickly. Niche hashtags (under 100K posts) have smaller but more targeted audiences where your post stays visible much longer and is discovered by genuinely interested people.
Can I use the same hashtags on every post?
Repeating identical hashtags can cause Instagram's algorithm to flag your account as spammy. Rotate between 3-4 different hashtag sets related to your niche. This generator creates varied hashtag sets each time to help with this.
Do hashtags work on LinkedIn?
Yes. LinkedIn hashtags help categorize your content and make it discoverable. Use 3-5 professional, industry-specific hashtags. LinkedIn suggests following hashtags, so your content can appear in followers' feeds.
Are there banned hashtags I should avoid?
Yes. Instagram periodically bans or restricts hashtags that have been associated with spam or inappropriate content. Using banned hashtags can suppress your post visibility. This tool avoids known banned hashtags in its suggestions.
